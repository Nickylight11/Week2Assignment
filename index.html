Lacrosse: A World Sport

Lacrosse, which originated as the "Medicine Game", amongst its creators, the Native Americans, has grown exponentially throughout the world.
The Native Americans used this game, beginning around the 12th Century, for ritualistic and war training purposes. There are individuals or
stickmakers who specialize in the art of shaping lacrosse sticks in the ancient methods, by carving hickory, string to craft the net, products of
animals and leather straps. Today, the game is enjoyed around the world, from youth (k-12), college, and professional atmospheres.
