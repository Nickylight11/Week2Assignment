History of Lacrosse

A Lacrosse Timeline

12th Century 1856 Late 1800s-Early 1900s 1932 2001 2006 2018
The Indigenous peoples of Native America started/played lacrosse prior to European contact. Montreal Lacrosse Club was founded in Canada. This would start organized lacrosse in North America. Lacrosse would begin its evolution within educational instituions which began leagues in schools and colleges. Lacrosse had been featured in the Summer Olympics in Los Angeles as a demonstration sport. International Lacrosse Federation or World Lacrosse as we know it today was established and worked to promote the sport on a global scale. Women's lacrosse was recognized in the World Lacrosse Championships as a full medal sport. Lacrosse was added to the 2022 Summer Youth Olympics, making it one step further to being an official Olympic Sport.

Source: Lacrosse Origin & History from World Lacrosse Official Website
